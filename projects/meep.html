Robot “MEEP” — In Progress

Project Goal
To construct a legged robot using a Raspberry Pi, servos, and a battery pack, with 3D-printed parts.

Initial Designs in PTC Creo

Detailed CAD in SolidWorks

Working Leg

Assembled Robot

Robot MEEP can stand, and when the chassis is supported its legs can move at the hip and knee. Reprinting is in progress to adjust weight distribution to allow the robot to walk.
