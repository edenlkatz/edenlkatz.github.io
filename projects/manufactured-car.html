Manufactured Car

Project Goal
To design and fabricate a model of an early 20th-century race car using silicon casting, injection molding, machining & CAM, and 3D printing.

SolidWorks CAD and Casting of Car Mold

Machining and Injection Mold of Wheels

Assembled Car

The silicon-casted chassis was fitted with a 3D-printed cockpit and axles, on which injection-molded wheels were fastened.
